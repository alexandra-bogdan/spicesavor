Red Meat

Beef Stroganoff

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound (450g) beef sirloin, cut into thin strips
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 8 ounces (225g) mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish
  • Cooked egg noodles or rice, for serving

Instructions:

1.Sear the Beef:
  • Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the beef strips and cook until browned on all sides. Remove the beef from the skillet and set aside.
2.Cook the Vegetables:
  • In the same skillet, add another tablespoon of olive oil if needed. Add the chopped onion and cook until translucent. Add the minced garlic and sliced mushrooms. Cook until the mushrooms are tender and any liquid has evaporated.
3.Make the Sauce:
  • Sprinkle the flour over the mushrooms and stir to coat them evenly.
  • Gradually pour in the beef broth, stirring constantly to avoid lumps.
  • Add the Dijon mustard and Worcestershire sauce.
  • Let the mixture simmer and thicken, stirring occasionally.
4.Add Sour Cream:
  • Reduce the heat to low. Stir in the sour cream until the sauce is smooth and well combined.
  • Be careful not to let it boil, as sour cream can curdle at high temperatures. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
5.Combine with Beef:
  • Return the cooked beef strips to the skillet and simmer for a few minutes in the sauce until the beef is heated through and coated with the creamy mushroom mixture.
6.Serve:
  • Remove from heat. Serve the Beef Stroganoff over cooked egg noodles or rice. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley for a burst of color and added flavor.

Fish

Baked Lemon Garlic Butter Tilapia

Ingredients:

  • 4 tilapia fillets (or any white fish of your choice)
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon, juiced and zested
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano (or fresh oregano, chopped)
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish
  • Lemon slices, for garnish

Instructions:

1.Prepare the Oven:
  • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a baking dish with a little oil or cooking spray to prevent sticking.
2.Prepare the Lemon Garlic Butter:
  • In a small bowl, combine the melted butter, minced garlic, lemon juice, lemon zest, and dried oregano. Mix well.
3.Season the Fish:
  • Pat the tilapia fillets dry with paper towels.
  • Season both sides of the fillets with salt and black pepper.
4.Bake the Fish:
  • Place the seasoned tilapia fillets in the greased baking dish.
  • Pour the lemon garlic butter mixture evenly over the fillets.
5.Bake in the oven:
  • Bake the fish in the preheated oven for about 15-20 minutes or until the fish easily flakes with a fork and is opaque in the center.
  • Cooking time may vary based on the thickness of your fillets.
6.Garnish and Serve:
  • Once the fish is done, remove it from the oven.
  • Sprinkle freshly chopped parsley over the top for a burst of color and freshness.
  • Garnish with lemon slices for an extra touch of citrus flavor.
7.Serve Hot:
  • Serve the Baked Lemon Garlic Butter Tilapia hot, with your choice of side dishes such as steamed vegetables, rice, or a fresh salad.

Vegan

Vegan Chickpea and Vegetable Curry

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 cup diced carrots
  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped, for garnish
  • Cooked rice or naan bread, for serving

Instructions:

1.Sauté the Aromatics:
  • Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  • Add the chopped onion, minced garlic, and grated ginger. Sauté for a few minutes until the onions become translucent and fragrant.
2.Add Vegetables and Spices:
  • Add the diced red bell pepper, zucchini, and carrots to the pan.
  • Sauté for another 5 minutes until the vegetables begin to soften.
  • Add the curry powder, ground cumin, ground coriander, turmeric, salt, and black pepper.
  • Stir well to coat the vegetables with the spices.
3.Stir in Chickpeas and Tomatoes:
  • Add the drained chickpeas and diced tomatoes to the pan.
  • Stir to combine everything.
4.Simmer in Coconut Milk:
  • Pour in the coconut milk, and stir to mix it with the other ingredients.
  • Bring the mixture to a simmer, then reduce the heat to low.
  • Cover the pan and let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender and the flavors meld together.
5.Adjust Seasoning:
  • Taste the curry and adjust the seasoning, adding more salt, pepper, or spices if desired.
6.Serve:
  • Serve the Vegan Chickpea and Vegetable Curry over cooked rice or with naan bread
  • Garnish with freshly chopped cilantro for a burst of freshness.
