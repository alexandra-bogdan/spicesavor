Balanced Diet Meal

Grilled Chicken Salad

Ingredients
  • Grilled chicken breast (200g)
  • Mixed salad greens (lettuce, spinach, arugula) (2 cups)
  • Cherry tomatoes (1 cup)
  • Cucumber slices (1/2 cup)
  • Red bell pepper strips (1/2 cup)
  • Olive oil (1 tablespoon)
  • Balsamic vinegar (1 tablespoon)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

1.Season the chicken breast with salt and pepper and grill until fully cooked.

2. In a large bowl, mix the salad greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber slices, and red bell pepper strips.

3.Slice the grilled chicken breast and place it on top of the salad.

4.In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper to create the dressing.

5.Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.


Nutrition Information for Grilled Chicken Salad

Nutrient Amount
Calories 400 kcal
Protein 40g
Carbohydrates 15g
Dietary Fiber 5g
Sugars 7g
Fat 20g
Saturated Fat 3g
Cholesterol 100mg
Sodium 500mg
