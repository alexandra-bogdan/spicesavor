Classic Continental Breakfast

ingredients

  • Assorted pastries (croissants, pain au chocolat, danishes)
  • Sliced baguette or bread
  • Butter and fruit preserves
  • Fresh fruits (grapes, berries, melon slices)
  • Yogurt with honey or fruit compote
  • Cheese selection (brie, cheddar, or other favorites)
  • Cold cuts (ham, salami, or smoked salmon)
  • Hard-boiled eggs
  • Coffee or tea
  • Orange juice or other fruit juices

Instructions

1. Place an assortment of pastries on a serving platter or basket. You can include croissants, pain au chocolat, and danishes.

2. Provide sliced baguette or other types of bread. Place them in a basket alongside butter and a selection of fruit preserves.

3. Arrange a variety of fresh fruits on a platter. Consider grapes, berries, and melon slices for a colorful and refreshing touch.

4. Serve yogurt in individual bowls, and provide toppings such as honey or fruit compote. Guests can customize their yogurt parfaits.

5. Arrange a cheese selection on a platter, along with cold cuts like ham, salami, or smoked salmon.

6. Prepare and peel hard-boiled eggs. Serve them whole or sliced on a plate.

7. Set up a coffee station with a selection of coffee and tea. Provide milk, sugar, and other condiments. Include a refreshing beverage option like orange juice or another fruit juice.

8. Arrange all the components on a breakfast table or buffet, allowing guests to serve themselves and customize their breakfast plates.
